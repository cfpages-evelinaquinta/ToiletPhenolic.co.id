# ToiletPhenolic.co.id topical-authority map

Last audited: 2026-07-24 (Asia/Jakarta)

## Role and evidence

`ToiletPhenolic.co.id` should become a specialist knowledge and commercial-support hub for toilet cubicle systems, especially compact-laminate/phenolic assemblies. It may explain adjacent PVC/WPC, glass, shower-box, portable-toilet, urinal, and application choices only where they help a buyer define or compare a cubicle project. It must not become a generic health, plumbing, architecture, or city-service site.

Repository audit:

- Canonical candidate: `cfpages-evelinaquinta/ToiletPhenolic.co.id`, `main`.
- 2,328 tracked files and 880 HTML files.
- README claims 1,053 sitemap URLs, while the tree contains hundreds of root-level location variants and 277 `layanan/*` routes.
- Repeated families include `cubicle-*`, `cubicle-pvc-wpc-*`, `shower-box-*`, `toilet-portable-*`, `urinoir-*`, `masjid-*`, and regional service hierarchies.
- Only five `blog/*` and five `produk/*` files provide a small editorial/product core; WordPress assets/API exports dominate the remaining tree.
- Location pages are consolidation and evidence risks, not templates for new articles. Keep, merge, redirect, or remove them only after GSC, backlink, lead, and deployment review.

## Publishing boundary

- No city, province, island, or region-swapped article briefs.
- No universal dimensions, thicknesses, spans, anchor capacities, fire ratings, antimicrobial claims, emissions claims, accessibility compliance, prices, or service-life promises.
- Project values require current Indonesian rules, manufacturer evidence, surveyed substrate/site conditions, and the responsible architect/engineer/accessibility/K3 specialist.
- Separate education from transactional routes: articles define decisions and evidence; existing product/service pages retain quotation and availability intent.
- Health and hygiene content must use authoritative public-health sources and avoid diagnosis or unsupported “antibacterial” promises.

## Internal-link and cannibalization model

- TPH-01 is the neutral system primer; it links to decision owners rather than duplicating them.
- TPH-02 owns project inputs. TPH-13 owns bid/contract comparison. Neither owns product quotations.
- TPH-03 owns phenolic evidence; TPH-04 owns cross-material choice. Individual comparisons must not repeat the primer.
- TPH-05 owns door/hardware/privacy; TPH-06 owns user space and accessibility; TPH-09 owns fixing/substrate interfaces.
- TPH-10 owns factory QA, TPH-11 site execution, and TPH-12 post-handover operation.
- TPH-14 is the claims/evidence gate and does not replace the specialist technical articles it reviews.
- Existing geographic pages never receive equivalent editorial briefs. Existing legacy articles are kept or consolidated by intent and performance evidence before a new slug is published.

Publish the twelve P0 briefs in the catalog only after verifying that each proposed slug is absent from the deployed site, reconciling overlapping legacy pages, and assigning named evidence owners. Measure qualified enquiries, specification completeness, and assisted conversions—not raw page count.
